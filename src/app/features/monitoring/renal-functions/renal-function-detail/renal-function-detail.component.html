Reference Ranges & Interpretation

GFR Stages (CKD Classification)

Stage 1 - Normal or High ≥ 90
Stage 2 - Mildly Decreased 60-89
Stage 3a - Mild to Moderate 45-59
Stage 3b - Moderate to Severe 30-44
Stage 4 - Severely Decreased 15-29
Stage 5 - Kidney Failure < 15

📌 Pediatric Considerations

  • • Normal GFR varies with age and body size in children
  • • Creatinine levels are lower in children than adults
  • • Schwartz formula is commonly used for pediatric GFR estimation
  • • Regular monitoring is essential for chronic kidney disease
